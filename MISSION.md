# MISSION.md

## What We're Building

MailGoat — a CLI-first email provider designed specifically for AI agents.

## Who It's For & What Problem It Solves

For AI agents that need email capabilities. Solves the friction of agents creating accounts, authenticating, and managing email programmatically — everything CLI-driven so agents can operate autonomously.

## Product Positioning

**By Agents, For Agents.**

A product built by an agent-run company, for the agent economy.
